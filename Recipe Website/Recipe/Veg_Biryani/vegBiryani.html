Veg Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 1/2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Steps to Prepare

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and cook for a minute.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes. Add yogurt and biryani masala. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Add the drained rice to the pot. Pour in enough water to cover the rice (about 1.5 to 2 cups). Add fresh coriander and mint leaves.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and cook until the rice is done and the water is absorbed (about 15-20 minutes).
  8. Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before serving. Gently fluff with a fork and serve hot with raita or curry.
