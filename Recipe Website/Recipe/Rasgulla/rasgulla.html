Rasgulla Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tbsp rose water (optional)

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil the milk and add lemon juice to curdle it. Strain the curdled milk through a muslin cloth to obtain chhena (cottage cheese).
  2. Knead the chhena until smooth. Form small balls out of the dough.
  3. Prepare the sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water together. Add cardamom powder and rose water (if using).
  4. Gently add the chhena balls to the syrup. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the balls are cooked and have absorbed the syrup.
  5. Let it cool and soak in the syrup for a few hours before serving.
