Jalebi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, rice flour, yogurt, and baking powder. Mix well to form a smooth batter. Let it sit for 2 hours to ferment.
  2. In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Heat until the sugar dissolves. Add cardamom powder and let it simmer for 5 minutes to form a sugar syrup. Set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Fill a piping bag with the fermented batter. Cut a small hole in the tip of the piping bag.
  4. Pipe the batter into the hot oil in a spiral or circular motion to form jalebis. Fry until golden and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  5. Dip the hot jalebis into the warm sugar syrup for a few seconds. Remove and let cool on a wire rack.
  6. Serve hot or at room temperature. Enjoy your homemade jalebis!
