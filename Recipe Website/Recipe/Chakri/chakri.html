Chakri Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1/2 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a large bowl, mix rice flour, besan, sesame seeds, chili powder, turmeric powder, ajwain, cumin seeds, and salt.
  2. Add curd to the mixture and knead to form a soft dough. If the dough is too dry, add a little water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan for frying.
  4. Fill a chakri maker with the dough and press it out into the hot oil to form spirals.
  5. Fry the chakris until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  6. Allow them to cool before serving. Enjoy your homemade chakris!
