Egg Ghotala Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil separates.
  4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add peas and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Break the eggs directly into the pan and scramble them with the vegetables. Cook until the eggs are well cooked and mixed with the spices.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and mix thoroughly.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with bread or rice.
