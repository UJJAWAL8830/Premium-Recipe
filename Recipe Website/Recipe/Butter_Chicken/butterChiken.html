Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, boneless and cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Oil for cooking

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Stir in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink and are well coated with the masala.
  6. Reduce the heat and stir in the heavy cream and garam masala. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is creamy.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
