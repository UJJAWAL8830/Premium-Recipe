Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas (chole)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: turmeric, cumin seeds, coriander powder, garam masala
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (for Bhature)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Oil for frying

Steps to Prepare

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight. Boil them with salt and a pinch of baking soda until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds, onion, and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until golden.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add boiled chickpeas and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. For Bhature, knead the dough with flour, yogurt, and a pinch of salt. Let it rest for 2 hours.
  6. Roll the dough into circles and deep-fry until golden and puffed.
  7. Serve hot with chole, garnished with coriander.
