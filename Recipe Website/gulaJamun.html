Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk (or as needed)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda. Add ghee and mix well.
  2. Gradually add milk to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. While the dough is resting, prepare the sugar syrup. In a pan, combine sugar, water, and cardamom powder. Heat until the sugar dissolves. Let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a pan for frying.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls. Fry them in the hot oil or ghee until they are golden brown. Make sure to fry on low heat to ensure they cook through.
  6. Remove the fried balls and immediately place them in the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 1 hour.
  7. Serve the Gulab Jamun warm or at room temperature.
